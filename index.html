Our history

Created in 2018 by its young founder Benoit Raillard-Cazanove, a graduate of HEC Paris, and his associates, La Chaussetterie was born above all from a shared passion for socks, whether handmade or industrial. The adventure started quietly in the small town of Aix-en-Provence before the Chaussetterie, strengthened by its success, set up in several large cities such as Marseille, Paris and Lyon.

Our goals

La Chaussetterie's main aim is to respond to the growing demand from individuals for local, good quality and affordable textiles. That is why, thanks to the expertise of our employees and our craftsmen, our objective is to combine comfort, quality and durability and all this at the lowest possible price.

Our products

La Chaussetterie works hand in hand with the most renowned craftsmen in the sock world, such as Sylvio Enrique and Mathias Copuni, which allows us to offer sock models that are always at the cutting edge of fashion, depending on the style desired. Our sports socks are also tested in laboratories by our scientific teams in order to maximise their adherence and thus maximise our customers' performance. Finally, our team is in contact with the best specialists in terms of knowledge of the most resistant materials.

Our partnerships

Already recognized as a pillar in the hostile world of socks, La Chaussetterie has been able to carry out multiple collaborations during temporary collections, with influential brands in their field of choice, such as LVMH, SUPREME, but also GORE-TEX or CONVERSE.
